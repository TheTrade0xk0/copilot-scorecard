How to find your Anthropic API Key

1
Go to console.anthropic.com and sign up or log in.
2
In the left sidebar, click API Keys.
3
Click Create Key, give it a name, and copy it. It starts with sk-ant-.
Open Anthropic Console ↗

How to find your Colosseum PAT

1

2
Click Step 1 to generate your Personal Access Token.
3
Copy the token and paste it into the field. It starts with eyJ.
